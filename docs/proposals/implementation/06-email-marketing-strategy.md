## Part 1: Email Capture

### Lead Magnets (Ranked by Value)

#### 1. Free Sample Request (PRIMARY)

**Why it works:**
- Straws and cups are tactile products - buyers want to feel quality
- Extremely high intent - only serious buyers request samples
- Natural conversation starter for sales follow-up
- Low cost to fulfill (Â£1-2 per sample pack)

**Form fields:**
- Business name (required)
- Contact name (required)
- Email (required)
- Phone (optional)
- Products interested in (checkboxes)
- Estimated monthly volume (dropdown)
- Delivery address (required)

**Where to promote:**
- Prominent button on all product pages
- Header announcement bar
- Dedicated landing page for ads

---

#### 2. Custom Quote Request (HIGH INTENT)

**Why it works:**
- Large orders need personalized pricing
- Shows serious purchase intent
- Creates sales conversation opportunity

**Form fields:**
- Business name
- Contact name
- Email
- Phone
- Products needed (text area)
- Estimated quantities
- Delivery location
- Timeline (when do they need it?)

**Where to promote:**
- Product pages (for bulk quantities)
- Dedicated "Trade" or "Wholesale" page
- Exit intent for visitors viewing multiple products

---

#### 3. Newsletter Signup (LOW FRICTION)

**Why it works:**
- Catches visitors not ready to buy yet
- Minimal commitment
- Builds long-term audience

**Form fields:**
- Email only
- Optional: Business type (cafe, restaurant, catering, events, other)

**Where to promote:**
- Footer (every page)
- Blog posts
- End of content pages

**Value proposition examples:**
- "Join 500+ UK hospitality businesses"
- "Get eco packaging tips, new products, and exclusive offers"
- "Monthly sustainability insights for hospitality"

---

#### 4. Gated Content (USE SPARINGLY)

Only gate content that provides genuine value not available elsewhere.

**Worth gating:**
- "UK Packaging Regulations 2024: What Hospitality Businesses Need to Know"
- "Case Study: How [Real Business] Reduced Packaging Costs 30%"
- "Paper vs PLA vs Bagasse: Independent Comparison Report"

**Not worth gating:**
- Generic "eco tips" PDF
- Price lists (already on website)
- Basic product guides

## Part 6: Attribution & Revenue Share

### Tracking Email-Attributed Revenue

Every email should include UTM parameters:

```
https://afida.com/products/paper-straws?utm_source=email&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=welcome_sequence&utm_content=email_3
```

**UTM structure for email:**
- `utm_source`: `email`
- `utm_medium`: `email`
- `utm_campaign`: Sequence or campaign name
- `utm_content`: Specific email (optional)

### Attribution Window

- Customer clicks email link
- UTM params stored in session
- If they purchase within 7 days, order attributed to email

### Monthly Revenue Share Calculation

```sql
-- Email-attributed revenue
SELECT
  CASE
    WHEN product_category = 'straws' THEN 'straws'
    ELSE 'other'
  END as category,
  SUM(total_amount) as revenue,
  CASE
    WHEN product_category = 'straws' THEN SUM(total_amount) * 0.20
    ELSE SUM(total_amount) * 0.12
  END as your_share
FROM orders
WHERE attribution_source = 'email'
  AND created_at >= '2025-01-01'
  AND created_at < '2025-02-01'
GROUP BY 1;
```
