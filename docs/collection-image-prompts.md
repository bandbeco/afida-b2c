# Collection Image Generation Prompts

Prompts for generating collection hero images using an image generation LLM (e.g., Google's Imagen).

Each prompt can be used for both the main collection and its matching sample pack.

---

## 1. Coffee Shops / Coffee Shop Sample Pack

```
Professional product photography of eco-friendly coffee shop supplies arranged on a rustic wooden counter. Feature single-wall and double-wall paper coffee cups in various sizes (8oz, 12oz, 16oz) with kraft brown and white finishes, matching sip lids, wooden stirrers, and a cardboard cup carrier holding four cups. Soft morning light from the left, shallow depth of field, warm tones. Clean, minimal composition with a blurred coffee shop background. No text or logos on products.
```

## 2. Bakeries / Bakery Sample Pack

```
Professional product photography of bakery packaging supplies arranged on a marble countertop dusted with flour. Feature kraft paper bags (flat handle style), paper coffee cups with lids, dispenser napkins, and pastry boxes. Include a few croissants and muffins as props to suggest bakery context. Warm golden morning light, soft shadows, cozy atmosphere. Neutral cream and brown color palette. No text or logos visible.
```

## 3. Ice Cream Parlours / Ice Cream Parlour Sample Pack

```
Vibrant product photography of ice cream parlour supplies on a bright, playful background. Feature colourful paper ice cream cups in various sizes, wooden spoons, and cocktail napkins in cheerful colours. Include scoops of colourful ice cream (strawberry, mint, vanilla) as props. Bright, even lighting with soft shadows. Fun, summery atmosphere with pastel pink and mint green accents. No text or logos on products.
```

## 4. Restaurants / Restaurant Sample Pack

```
Professional product photography of restaurant takeaway packaging on a dark slate surface. Feature rectangular and round kraft bowls with clear lids, flat handle paper bags, takeaway boxes, cocktail napkins, and coffee cups. Elegant, upscale presentation suggesting quality dining. Dramatic side lighting with deep shadows, sophisticated mood. Earth tones - kraft brown, black, and white. No text or logos visible.
```

## 5. Takeaway / Takeaway Sample Pack

```
Professional product photography of complete takeaway packaging set on a rustic pizzeria counter with red-and-white checkered details. Feature pizza boxes (various sizes), soup containers with lids, kraft food boxes, flat handle bags, and disposable cutlery. Warm overhead pendant lights, wood-fired pizza oven blurred in background. Styled as if ready for a busy Friday night delivery rush. Warm, inviting atmosphere with terracotta and kraft brown tones. No text or logos on packaging.
```

## 6. Smoothie & Juice Bars / Smoothie & Juice Bar Sample Pack

```
Fresh, vibrant product photography of smoothie bar supplies. Feature crystal-clear PET cups filled with colourful smoothies (green, berry, orange), dome and flat lids, and eco-friendly paper straws in various colours. Tropical fruits (mango, berries, kiwi) scattered as props. Bright, natural daylight, white background with colourful accents. Healthy, fresh, energetic mood. No text or logos visible.
```

Include a cocktail glass and beer tap handle blurred in background for context. Warm amber lighting suggesting evening atmosphere, shallow depth of field. Sophisticated, nightlife aesthetic. No text or logos visible.
```

## 8. Hotels (Collection only)

```
Elegant product photography of premium hotel service supplies on a pristine white linen surface. Feature double-wall coffee cups with lids, cloth-like dispenser napkins, wooden stirrers, and coffee cup carriers. Silver tray and white porcelain as props. Soft, diffused lighting suggesting luxury. ## 9. Eco Essentials (Collection only)

```
Natural, earthy product photography of sustainable packaging supplies on a bed of fresh green leaves and moss. Feature bamboo straws, wooden coffee stirrers, kraft paper cups with PLA lids, and compostable containers. Soft natural daylight filtering through foliage, green and brown earth tones. Eco-friendly, nature-focused aesthetic emphasising sustainability. Dewdrops on leaves for freshness. No text or logos visible.
```

---

## Usage Notes

- All prompts designed for commercial product photography style
- "No text or logos" instruction prevents AI from generating fake branding
- Props (food items, drinks) add context without being the focus
- Lighting and surface choices match the mood of each business type
- Aspect ratio recommendation: 16:9 or 3:2 for hero images
