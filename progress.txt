## Key Decisions
- Data model: self-referential Category with parent_id
- Vegware: Collection (brand filter), not a category
- Nav: two bars, hover-to-open mega-menu (deviates from PRD which specifies click; user preference)
- Mobile: hamburger drill-down, pill bar removed, account links included
- URLs: /categories/:parent/:subcategory, 301 redirects from old URLs
- Shop All: keep existing /shop page, no icon grid
- Icons: SVGs top-level only, text-only subcategories
- 7 real categories + 1 collection (Vegware), 27 subcategories total
- Supplies & Essentials replaces Takeaway Extras (kitchen ops items)
- Sugar sticks -> Supplies & Essentials
- Branded Packaging: show only what exists (Branded Cups, Branded Greaseproof)
